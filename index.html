Cat Videos

Cat videos are a timeless tradition on the internet. I personally love cats, and used my childhood internet access to watch funny videos of cats.
Below are several that have either stood out to me or made me laugh. They may even have interesting historical significance.

The first ever cat video, May 1900 (upscaled, colorized, speed adjusted)

This is a video of little Madeleine Koehler feeding and playing with the family cat, estimated to be recorded in May of 1900.
According to the video's discription, "This video is part the Lumiere Brothers' portfolio of over 1400 video clips, shot between 1895 and 1905."
This video is a cool piece of history involving one of humanities favorite furry creature.

Keyboard Cat! - THE ORIGINAL!

This is a very famous video, known as "Keyboard Cat." The video was recorded in 1984, and featured a female orange cat named
"Fatso" wearing a light blue shirt. According to the cat's owner and the video's creator Charlie Schmidt,
"I wasn't aiming for anything except relieving my boredom."

W&W - OIIA OIIA (Spinning Cat)

This is a more recent famous cat video, and it involves a blind rescue cat named "Ethel." A meme has been going around for months
of Ethel spinning over a green screen, and the video below is a remix of the original. It is a electric dance remix made by W&W, a Dutch DJ Duo.
